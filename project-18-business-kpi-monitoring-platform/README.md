# Project 18 – Business KPI Monitoring Platform

Enterprise Business KPI Monitoring Platform using Prometheus, Grafana, Alertmanager, and Executive Business Intelligence Dashboards.

---

# Overview

This project demonstrates enterprise-grade monitoring of business Key Performance Indicators (KPIs).

The platform provides executives, business leaders, and operational managers with real-time visibility into revenue performance, customer growth, operational efficiency, financial metrics, and strategic objectives.

The solution transforms operational data into business intelligence dashboards for executive decision-making.

---

# Business Scenario

Modern organizations depend on accurate KPI tracking to measure business performance.

Without centralized KPI monitoring organizations may experience:

- Delayed Decision Making
- Poor Visibility
- Revenue Leakage
- Customer Retention Issues
- Operational Inefficiencies
- Strategic Misalignment

This platform provides a single source of truth for business performance monitoring.
